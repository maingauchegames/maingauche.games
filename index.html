About

Main Gauche Games is an all-remote game studio founded in 2022 by a pair of Silicon Valley burnouts, Jeff Lee and Dominic Dagradi. They are both vegetarian, although sometimes Jeff cheats. The studio was established after two years of regular game dev streams on Twitch, out of which emerged approximately 0.46 games (cumulatively), alongside uncountable hours of armchair political theorizing.

Their first commercial release, Stereo Boy, is due to launch in summer 2022. Which is to say, as of this writing, imminently. It is an isometric sci-fi puzzle box that began as a deliberate attempt to avoid making a puzzle game. You know how these things go. Anyway, we think you will enjoy it.
